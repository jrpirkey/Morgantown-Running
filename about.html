Morgantown Running
EST. 2009

Originally opened in 2009 by owners Erin and Kevin Roush, Morgantown Running started in Westover, WV before moving to the Seneca Center in 2010. It was later purchased by Sean and Heather Mcdaniel Cleary who run it to this day

The Store

From runner to runner, our store is here to help you find exactly what you need. The reason we carry the products that we do? We use them too! We are always looking for the latest shoes and gear that we can rely on when running in the conditions that Morgantown graciously serves us year round.
As Morgantown's only specialty running store, we are here to offer you the best shoes and gear to fit your running needs. Our enthusiastic and well-trained staff is available to assist you no matter the type of walker or runner you are.

Meet our Staff

Heather McDaniel Clear

Heather joins the ranks of mothers around the world who are finding their athletic potential after having a child. After graduating as conference champion in both track and cross country with academic All-American honors from Alderson Broaddus college in Philippi, WV, Heather has found her running moments very recently.

Kelly Williams

Besides playing tag in the backyard, Kelly's first running memories come from middle school sprints, hurdles, and field events. Fast forward to college and she developed into distance-running nerd! A recent grad from WVU, she couldn't see herself leaving the Morgantown area and giving up her running goals and dreams just yet. Kelly is in charge of all things media and social for Morgantown Running.

Emily Bauman

The diatetic internship at WVU brought Emily to Morgantown, as she joins us all the way from Wisconsin where she was born and raised, and also where she went to undergrad. Formerly a collegiate runner, she is recently getting into training for a triathlon. A runner since the age of 12, Emily is naturally fit for our store and has the expertise to help find the right shoe for you!

Jenny Freitas

Jenny developed a love for running in middle school when she began running. She ran middle school and high school cross country and track for Grafton. It was during her exchange in Brazil that she ran several 10ks and fell in love with road racing. Now, Jenny is a student at WVU and runs for the WVU running club!
