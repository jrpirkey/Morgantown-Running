Morgantown Running Club

A Club for Youth Runners

Morgantown Running Club or MRC is a club for youth runners in middle school or younger that get together either once or twice a week to run at WVU Indoor track/ Shell building. At the end of each season we hold a meet for the students to compete and continue to progress towards their goals.
